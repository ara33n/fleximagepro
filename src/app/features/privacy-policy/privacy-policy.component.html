Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Overview

FlexImagePro is a browser-based image processing tool. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your privacy. The short version: your images never leave your device.

Image Processing

All image operations — compression, format conversion, and resizing — run entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and the File API. No image data is transmitted to any server. We have no access to the files you process, and they are not stored anywhere outside your local device memory.

Information We Collect

FlexImagePro does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or account credentials. We do not require registration to use any tool.

We may collect anonymised, aggregated usage data through standard web analytics (such as page views and general browser/OS information) to understand how the site is used and to improve performance. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Cookies

FlexImagePro may use minimal session storage and local storage to remember your tool preferences (such as dark mode) between visits. These are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to any third party. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes beyond what Google AdSense sets automatically (see below).

Third-Party Services

FlexImagePro uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Beyond Google AdSense, we do not share data with any other third parties for commercial purposes.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Any browser storage (local storage, session storage) used by FlexImagePro remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

FlexImagePro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of FlexImagePro after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@fleximagepro.com.
